A project portfolio is a curated collection of an organization's projects, providing a comprehensive overview of its initiatives, goals, and achievements. It serves as a strategic tool for decision-makers to assess the health and progress of individual projects and their collective impact on organizational objectives. The project portfolio typically includes key information such as project objectives, timelines, budgets, resource allocation, and risk assessments. By maintaining a well-managed project portfolio, businesses can prioritize, monitor, and optimize their projects to ensure alignment with overall business strategies.
